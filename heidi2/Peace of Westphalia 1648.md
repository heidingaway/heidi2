# Peace of Westphalia 1648

> They ended the [Thirty Years' War](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thirty_Years%27_War "Thirty Years' War") (1618–1648) and brought peace to the [Holy Roman Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Holy_Roman_Empire "Holy Roman Empire"), closing a calamitous period of European history that killed approximately eight million people. [Holy Roman Emperor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Holy_Roman_Emperor "Holy Roman Emperor") [Ferdinand III](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ferdinand_III,_Holy_Roman_Emperor "Ferdinand III, Holy Roman Emperor"), the kingdoms of [France](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_France "Kingdom of France") and [Sweden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Sweden_\(800%E2%80%931521\) "Kingdom of Sweden (800–1521)"), and their respective allies among the princes of the Holy Roman Empire, participated in the treaties.[^2]
