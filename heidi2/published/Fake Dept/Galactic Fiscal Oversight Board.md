# Galactic Fiscal Oversight Board (FOBS)
The **Galactic Fiscal Oversight Board** is responsible for providing **central oversight and direction** on how the Interstellar Public Administration operates, how it allocates its vast resources, and how it delivers services to Galactic citizens in the [[Government of the Galaxy]]. It is the primary body ensuring **fiscal accountability, administrative excellence, and optimal resource utilization** across all governmental entities.

FOBS is overseen by the [[President of the Galactic Fiscal Oversight Board]] and organizes its work across [[FOBS Business Lines]].
