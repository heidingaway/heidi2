# OCSRO Responses - Organization Info

[[OCSRO Office of the Chief Sentient Resources Officer]] is [[function]] within the [[Galactic Fiscal Oversight Board]].

OSCRO organizes its work across the [[OCSRO BusinessLines]].

OSCRO has [[OCSRO Stakeholders]] that interact with its business lines and outputs .

OCSRO produces [[OCSRO Outputs]] which drives the [[OCSRO Outcome]].

The [[Sentient Force Analytics and Reporting]] has submitted [[OCSRO Responses]] in the DMAT interface on behalf of the OCSRO.
