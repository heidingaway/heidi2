# Office of the Chief Sentient Resources Officer (OCSRO)

Reporting directly to the President of the **Galactic Fiscal Oversight Board**, the Office of the Chief Sentient Resources Officer provides **strategic, enterprise-wide leadership and functional direction for the _orchestration and optimization of Sentient Resources_ across the core Interstellar Public Administration.** In its capacity, the CSRO supports the Board of Fiscal Oversight in its role as the employer of the Interstellar Public Administration, ensuring excellence in the _algorithmic resource management_ of our vital sentient entities.

The CSRO is responsible for the development, implementation, and oversight of policies, directives, and standards governing the lifecycle and well-being of all recognized Sentient Resources within the federal government. This mandate is fulfilled in a manner compatible with primary deputy head accountability and responsibility for their respective departmental Sentient Resource complements.
