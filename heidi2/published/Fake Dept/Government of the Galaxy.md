# Government of the Galaxy

> The Government of the Galaxy (or, more formally, the Galactic Concordat) is a sprawling, multi-species, and increasingly multi-sentient, bureaucratic entity that governs a significant, though not entirely universal, portion of the known cosmos. It's a complex, often absurd, blend of ancient traditions, cutting-edge futuristic technologies, and a deep-seated commitment to maintaining an ordered, if occasionally baffling, existence for its billions of citizens.
