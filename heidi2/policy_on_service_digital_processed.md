# Policy on Service and Digital Processed

Policy on Service and Digital has supporting instruments.
Policy on Service and Digital serves as an integrated set of rules on service and digital.
Policy on Service and Digital applies to Government of Canada organizations.
Policy on Service and Digital defines how Government of Canada organizations manage service delivery.
Policy on Service and Digital defines how Government of Canada organizations manage information and data.
Policy on Service and Digital defines how Government of Canada organizations manage information technology.
Policy on Service and Digital defines how Government of Canada organizations manage cyber security.
Policy on Service and Digital responds to the digital era.
The Policy on Service and Digital defines how Government of Canada organizations manage service and digital functions.
service and digital functions includes service delivery, information and data, information management and cyber security.
Government of Canada organizations must follow requirements for managing service and digital functions in conjunction with requirements for privacy, requirements for official languages, and requirements for accessibility.
Requirements for privacy, requirements for official languages, and requirements for accessibility are set out in section 8 of the Policy on Service and Digital.
Government of Canada Organizations develop operations and services.
Policy on Service and Digital focuses on the client.
clients use operations and services.
Focusing on the client includes considering proactively key requirements when designing operations and services.
operations and services includes programs.
Key requirements include service and digital functions.
Policy on Service and Digital establishes an enterprise-wide approach to governance, planning, and management.
Policy on Service and Digital establishes an integrated approach to governance, planning, and management.
Policy on Service and Digital advances service delivery and effectiveness of government operations.
Advances service delivery and effectiveness of government operations includes strategic management of government information and data. 
Advances service delivery and effectiveness of government operations includes leveraging of information technology.
Advances service delivery and effectiveness of government operations includes supporting the mandate of the Minister for Digital Government.
Mandate of the Minister for Digital Government is to lead the Government of Canada’s digital transition.
The digital transition is a response to the digital era.
Service and digital functions are guided by the Government of Canada Digital Standards.
Government of Canada Digital Standards has parts: design with users; iterate and improve frequently; work in the open by default; use open standards and solutions; address security and privacy risks; build in accessible from the start; empower staff to deliver better services; be good data stewards; design ethical services; collaborate widely.
